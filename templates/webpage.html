How Clustering Works


Using Machine Learning powered by JURIST articles, we break down a broad topic (Ex. Facebook) into smaller subsets (Ex. Privacy, Antitrust) of articles that are displayed on timelines.


We believe that understanding the development of any broad and complex news topic shouldn't involve a time-consuming search process.
